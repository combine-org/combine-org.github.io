Elebeoba May (Ph.D in Computer Engineering) is an Associate Professor in the Department of Medical Microbiology & Immunology and the Wisconsin Institute for Discovery, and an investigator at the Center for Reproducible Biomedical Modeling and the Center for Theoretical Biological Physics.  Her group develops integrated experimental and computational multi-scale, predictive models of biological systems to study infection, microbial stress response, and multicellular communities. Her lab is involved in the development of tools to translate non-SBML metabolic models to SBML for reproducibile modeling.
